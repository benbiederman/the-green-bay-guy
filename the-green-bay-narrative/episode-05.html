Mark and Maggie

For this episode of The Green Bay Narrative, I throw a curveball at you. I sit down and interview a huge Packers fan and her husband, who happens to be a big-time Browns fan. During my conversation with Maggie and Mark, they share stories about how they became fans of their teams, their experience of attending a game in Cleveland, and getting married at Lambeau Field. Mark and Maggie shared some funny stories that are sure to entertain you.
