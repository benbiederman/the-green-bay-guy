On this week's episode of The Green Bay Narrative, I am joined by Robert, a die-hard Packers fan from Texas. During our conversation, we discuss the Bears vs. Packers matchup in Week 1 of the 2018 season, how he became a Packers fan, his first trip to Green Bay, his initial impression of the city, and much more. Robert was a fun guest to have; he made my job easy with his great storytelling.
