Matt Ramage

During this episode of The Green Bay Narrative, I sit down with the creator of Packers Memes, now better known as Matt Ramage. Recorded prior to his page name change, our conversation delves into what inspired him to start his page, his experience growing up in Green Bay, the time he hung out with Tom Crabtee, and much more. Matt and I go way back, so it was a pleasure to sit down and hear his stories. I hope you enjoy listening to it as much as I enjoyed recording it.
