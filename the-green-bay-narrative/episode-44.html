Steve 'The Owner' Tate

On this week's episode of The Green Bay Guy Narrative, Steve 'The Owner' joins the podcast. During our conversation, we talk about how he became a fan, how he turned into 'The Owner', his favorite game he's attended, and so much more. Steve's story was one I've been waiting to hear since I started the podcast, and I'm glad we can finally release it! It was an honor to hear Steve's stories.
