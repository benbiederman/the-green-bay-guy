Landon K.

For this episode of The Green Bay Narrative, I sit down and speak with one of the newest members of the Packers equipment team. Landon, originally from Illinois, is a die-hard Packer backer. In this episode, we delve into how he became a huge Packers fan despite being an Illinois native, his experiences at Packers training camp, game days, and much more. Landon is a super nice guy, and it was great to have the opportunity to hear his story. I hope you all enjoy it as much as I did.
