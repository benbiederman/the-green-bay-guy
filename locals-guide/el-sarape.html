El Sarape

Welcome to El Sarape, a Mexican restaurant renowned for its authentic Mexican dishes. Having opened its first location in 2000, El Sarape has proudly served the Green Bay area for over 20 years. Since the launch of the initial restaurant, El Sarape has expanded with another location, ensuring you are never too far from delicious Mexican food, no matter where you are in the Green Bay area.

When it comes to my go-to recommendations for Mexican food, El Sarape always makes that list. I am not alone in that recommendation either; El Sarape is known to be the favorite Mexican restaurant for many Packers players, both past and present. As you enter the location closest to Lambeau Field, you will spot a bench signed by numerous players who have dined at the establishment over the years.

What really sets El Sarape apart for me are three key factors: quality, consistency, and price. I have been a frequent visitor to El Sarape over the years and have never been disappointed. Every time I have placed an order, it has been not only correct but also accommodating to my somewhat picky taste buds. The food is consistently fresh, offering excellent quality at a reasonable price. Personally, I always go for a burrito when I visit El Sarape, and I've yet to finish one entirely. They truly provide great value for the money.

While there are several excellent Mexican restaurants in Green Bay, I cannot recommend El Sarape enough. Its combination of delicious food, reasonable pricing, and consistent quality makes it one of my top choices in Green Bay. I think you will enjoy it too.
