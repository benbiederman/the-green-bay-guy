Card and Coin

If you’re on the prowl for unique and/or vintage Packers items, look no further than Card and Coin. Card and Coin has been in the Green Bay area for over 20 years. They specialize in sports and trading cards, coin collecting, and especially Packers memorabilia.

Over the years, I’ve stopped by Card and Coin numerous times and always enjoy browsing the memorabilia they have to offer. It’s one of my favorite memorabilia stores simply because of the unique items they have for sale.

Another nice aspect of Card and Coin is its proximity to Lambeau Field. If you visited years ago, please note that they moved to a new location in 2019. Don’t fret, though – their new location is still a short distance from Lambeau Field, making it the perfect place to stop and shop.

Whether you’re looking to buy a pack of cards, a cool Ray Nitschke item, or simply want to browse their fantastic store, Card and Coin has it all – I definitely recommend checking them out!
