Commando Paintball Sports

Welcome to Commando Paintball Sports, a business outside of Green Bay specializing in gellyball, laser tag, airsoft, and, of course, paintball. Commando Paintball Sports offers a large playing area to ensure there's plenty of space for any game you choose to play.

Whether you're visiting Commando Paintball Sports in a group or individually, they can accommodate everyone. If your party has fewer than eight people, you can only play on the weekends. However, if you have a larger group, you can book a party and have more flexibility in scheduling your game. This makes it a perfect venue for bachelor parties, birthday parties, work outings, or even a fun way to determine the draft order for your next Fantasy Football league.

One aspect I really enjoy about Commando Paintball Sports is the different scenarios they set up for their customers. At the time of writing, upcoming events include a Star Wars scenario and a Zombie Outbreak scenario. These themed events add an exciting story element to the game, making it more than just another day on the paintball course.

A couple of things to note if you're planning a visit to Commando Paintball Sports: they are open all year long, but their hours change depending on the season. The hours listed below are for the summer, so if you're looking to play in the winter, check their website for current hours. Also, if you're planning to play in the winter, be sure to bundle up, as all games are played outdoors.

Overall, Commando Paintball Sports offers a fun and unique activity in the area. Whether you're a paintball veteran or a first-timer, Commando Paintball Sports is welcoming to all.
