Anduzzi's

With four locations in Wisconsin, three of which are in the Green Bay area, Anduzzi's has established itself as a staple in the region. Winning Best of the Bay as a bar in 2018, Anduzzi's has much to offer for those visiting the area.

The first thing I noticed when I went to Anduzzi's was the abundance of seating. Although the parking lot was nearly full, there was still plenty of seating inside. They offer an outdoor patio, which helped balance the number of people at the location.

While looking at the menu, I told myself I wasn't going to order a burger this time. I generally tend to get burgers when dining out, so I wanted to try something different. But then I saw it... a Spotted Cow Cheese Curd burger. This glorious burger was topped with bacon, Spotted Cow cheese sauce, and cheese curds. At that point, my plan to avoid a burger went out the window. I had to try it.

When it comes to the food, both my wife and I were pleasantly surprised by how good it was. The burger was fantastic, and the fries were surprisingly good. We left wondering why we don't venture to Anduzzi's more often.

If you're looking for a spot to have some drinks or grab some food near Lambeau Field, Anduzzi's would be a great choice. I'm looking forward to my next visit there.
