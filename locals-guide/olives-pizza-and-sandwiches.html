Olive's Pizza and Sandwiches

Welcome to Olive’s Pizza and Sandwiches, a restaurant that opened its doors in 2021. While it may be newer to the area, Olive’s has quickly built a name for itself by serving high-quality, delicious food.

If my love for pizza wasn’t enough to get me excited about trying Olive’s, I also learned that they named their restaurant after their beloved cat, Olive. I’m a sucker for cats and love to pspsps at them any chance I get. So this seemed like a great place for me to try. I should note that the animals do not go into the restaurant; it’s not a cat restaurant or anything like that – just an inspiration for the name. So fret not, the place is clean and cat hair free!

Full disclosure, I’ve never dined inside Olive’s, so I can’t speak to their in-restaurant service. However, the few times I’ve ordered from Olive’s, the workers have been incredibly friendly and very transparent with their communication. During my most recent visit, they were much busier than expected. They apologized profusely for the short wait and kept me informed throughout the entire process. While waiting was no issue for me, I appreciated knowing how long the wait would be and their positive attitude.

Let’s talk about why we’re all here – the food. I’ve tried both their thin crust and hand-tossed pies. The thin crust was decent, but it didn’t compare to their hand-tossed pizza. I’m a big fan of their dough, and the hand-tossed has a very nice chew. Their stone deck oven helps the dough come out nice and crispy on the outside but soft and chewy on the inside, making it a great experience.

Along with Olive’s delicious pizza, they also offer a wide variety of sandwiches. This shouldn’t be too much of a surprise as their name is Olive’s Pizza and Sandwiches. While I haven’t had any of their sandwiches yet, I’ve heard great things about them from others who have tried them.

Overall, my experiences at Olive’s have been enjoyable, and in my opinion, you can never have too many great pizza places in the area. I’m very glad to have Olive’s Pizza and Sandwiches in the Green Bay community. If you’re looking for a nice pizza place in the area, give them a try.
