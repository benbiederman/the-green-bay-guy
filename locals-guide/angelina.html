Angelina

Craving an unforgettable meal in Green Bay? Look no further than Angelina, where you'll experience the true essence of authentic Italian cuisine. Founded by a passionate cook who hails from Italy, Angelina brings a delightful taste of Italy right to your table, leaving you utterly amazed. Having explored Italy myself, I can confidently say that Angelina remains one of my favorite Italian dining experiences I've encountered.

Expecting a crowd? You're not alone! Angelina is a hotspot in Green Bay, particularly on Fridays and Saturdays. Beat the rush by making a reservation; it's a surefire way to guarantee an unforgettable culinary adventure at Angelina.

If you're stopping by Angelina during one of the few months of decent weather in Wisconsin, be sure to take advantage of their incredible outdoor seating area behind the building. If the weather permits and they have space available, I highly recommend dining outside.

Their dishes play a trick on you with their deceptive sizes. Whenever I dine at Angelina, I anticipate leaving hungry, but I'm consistently proven wrong! Their in-house-made fresh pasta leaves us so full that indulging in dessert becomes an impossible mission. The ravioli in their bolognese meat sauce is a revelation that leaves me wanting more.

Hands down, if you're on the hunt for an exceptional dinner spot, Angelina is at the top of my recommendations. Trust me; you won't regret making this choice. I'm already counting down the days until my next visit.
