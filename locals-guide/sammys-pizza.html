Sammy's Pizza

Less than a mile from Lambeau Field, you'll find Sammy's Pizza, a family-owned and operated Italian restaurant. Widely regarded as a favorite in the community, Sammy's Pizza takes pride in serving homemade, high-quality dishes crafted with fresh ingredients.

Get ready to embark on an exceptional dining experience when you enter the doors of Sammy's. Although the prices might be slightly higher than some other pizza places, the undeniable quality more than makes up for it. It's no surprise that Sammy's has garnered a loyal following spanning generations since its establishment in the 1940s. With unwavering pride, they continue to uphold their cherished recipes, which are evident in every delectable slice of their extraordinary pizza.

Planning to catch a Packers game? Well, here's an unbeatable deal exclusively at Sammy's! Conveniently park your vehicle at Sammy's during game day, and as a delightful bonus, following the game, you can use your parking receipt towards food at Sammy's Pizza equivalent to the cost of your parking, allowing you to indulge in their delicious pizza while celebrating a Packers win.

Now, let's tackle the topic everyone wonders about: the price. But fear not, Sammy's Pizza may be a tad higher in price, yet it remains far from extravagant. When you take into account the exceptional quality, every penny becomes a worthwhile investment. Personally, I was astounded by the remarkable excellence of their pizza, which left me eagerly counting down the days until my next visit to Sammy's for another delightful pie.

When it comes to finding the perfect combination of authentic flavors, exceptional service, and a cozy atmosphere, Sammy's Pizza in Green Bay surpasses expectations. Whether you're a die-hard football fan seeking a post-game feast or simply a food lover craving extraordinary pizza, Sammy's is the place to be. Embrace the rich tradition and treat yourself to an unforgettable dining experience at Sammy's Pizza, where quality and taste take center stage.
