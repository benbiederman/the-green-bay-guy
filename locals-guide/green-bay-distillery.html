Green Bay Distillery

Discover Green Bay Distillery, a joint just a short stroll away from Lambeau Field in the heart of the Green Bay area. This popular spot is renowned for its mouthwatering Wisconsin style dishes, featuring must-try delights such as the delectable cheesecurd burger, tantalizing pretzel served with beer cheese, and the mouthwatering tailgater pizza. And to make the experience even more special, they offer an impressive selection of locally brewed beers that perfectly complement their dishes.

Whether you're a food enthusiast or looking for a great place to unwind with friends and family, Green Bay Distillery has something for everyone. Their menu boasts a delightful array of options, catering to various tastes, and it's expertly paired with a thoughtfully curated drink menu.

But the excitement doesn't stop there. Throughout the year, Green Bay Distillery hosts a diverse range of events that cater to different interests. If you're a Packers fan, you're in for a treat during the football season, as they organize exciting events like Inside the Huddle or the Kenny Clark Show.

Green Bay Distillery is truly a great joint in the Green Bay area. With its delectable dishes, enticing drink selection, and a vibrant calendar of events, it's a destination worth experiencing. Don't miss the chance to savor the authentic flavors of Wisconsin and create unforgettable memories at Green Bay Distillery.
