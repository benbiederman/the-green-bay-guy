Happy Joe's

Much like Joe, anytime I get the opportunity to eat pizza, I'm happy. In the heart of the East side of Green Bay, you'll find Happy Joe's, a pizza joint that stands out for its diverse crust options and tantalizing Mexican-inspired pizzas. On my recent visit, I opted for a classic pepperoni pizza for the ultimate taste-test round.

Upon the first delectable bite at Happy Joe's, I immediately noticed that their tomato sauce had a unique twist compared to other traditional pizzerias. As someone with a palate as simple as a 7-year-old's, I must admit that the sauce didn't quite harmonize with the pizza we ordered. It felt somewhat out of place, leaving me wondering if it would pair better with their Mexican-inspired pizza offerings. Perhaps it's an acquired taste that I have yet to fully appreciate.

While Happy Joe's pizza wasn't bad by any means, it simply differed from my personal sauce preferences. If I had to choose between Happy Joe's and Rustique Pizzeria, another popular pizza spot, I would lean towards the latter due to my sauce style preference. Nonetheless, I am open to giving Happy Joe's another shot, even though it might not become my top pizza destination.

Next time you're craving pizza in Green Bay, venture over to Happy Joe's and explore their array of crust choices and distinctive pizza flavors. Who knows, it might become your new favorite pizza place in town!
