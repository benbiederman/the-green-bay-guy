NEW Zoo

Located just outside of Green Bay in Suamico is the Northeastern Wisconsin (NEW) Zoo. The NEW Zoo is open 365 days a year, so you can get your zoo fix any time.

The NEW Zoo is a staple in our household. It’s one of our favorite places to take our son when we want to get out of the house and do something fun. The zoo offers a nice variety of animals and great paths that make it easy to navigate, whether you're walking or using a stroller.

Like most zoos, the NEW Zoo has a place for visitors to grab food or drinks. However, as with many other zoos, the prices are a bit steeper. The NEW Zoo also has a sizable gift shop where you can pick up a nice souvenir for the kids—just what you needed!

One tip: Depending on your family size or how often you plan on visiting, consider becoming a zoo member. A membership costs $95 for an entire family and offers many benefits, including discounts to other zoos around the country.
