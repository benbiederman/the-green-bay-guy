LoCo WisCo

If you find yourself in Green Bay and are looking for a memento of your trip or the perfect Wisconsin item, look no further than LoCo WisCo. Since 2022, LoCo WisCo has proudly sold items from Wisconsin-based companies.

The first thing that caught my attention as I walked into LoCo WisCo was the distinct Wisconsin vibe it exuded. The store is not only filled with great, unique Wisconsin-themed items, but it also plays Polka music, enhancing the full Wisconsin experience as you shop.

While browsing the many Wisconsin-themed products, one feature I absolutely loved was their "guest book." In a corner of the store, LoCo WisCo has painted the walls matte black, allowing guests to sign their names. Some visitors include the date and where they're from, showcasing how far people have traveled to visit.

My wife and I left LoCo WisCo feeling grateful that we have something like this in our town. While Green Bay offers plenty of Green Bay Packers souvenirs, it has often lacked non-Packers-themed options, and LoCo WisCo perfectly fills that gap. It's definitely worth a stop.
