Kroll's West

Looking for a classic dining experience near Lambeau Field in Green Bay? Look no further than Kroll's West, a beloved local joint serving up mouthwatering butter burgers and cheese curds. Conveniently situated next to the famous stadium, this restaurant has become a go-to spot for locals and tourists alike.

Step inside Kroll's, and you'll be transported back in time to a charming old-school diner. The cozy atmosphere features round booths and speedy wait staff, ready to make your dining experience enjoyable. Keep an eye out for a unique touch – a helpful button at your table for calling the service staff. Don't be shy to press it if you need anything!

One of the highlights of Kroll's is the nostalgic decor. Vintage Packer memorabilia adorns the walls, and they proudly display a collection of autographed cards from various celebrities. It's a treat to immerse yourself in the rich history of this cherished eatery.

Of course, the star attractions at Kroll's West are their scrumptious butter burgers and delectable cheese curds. Here's a fun fact – they offer a delightful variety of cheese curds, letting you choose between white, yellow, or a mix of both. It's a cheese lover's paradise!

I know this might be controversial to say, but I must admit that I'm not the biggest fan of Kroll's West. While I'd recommend it to frequent Green Bay visitors, it's essential to consider that it can be a bit pricier than some other options in the area. Its location near Lambeau Field naturally draws many tourists, especially during game times, which contributes to its popularity and slightly higher prices. Consequently, it tends to get quite busy, which can impact the quality and speed of service. For those who want to experience Kroll's West at least once, it is still worth a try. However, if you're like me and prefer heartier burgers at a more affordable price, you might want to explore Basil's II or Drift Inn, where you can find more substantial options that won't break the bank.

Green Bay boasts an array of fantastic dining spots, so choose wisely depending on your preferences. That said, a visit to Kroll's West for their scrumptious cheese curds is an acceptable choice in my eyes.
