Welcome to Chives, a restaurant located just outside of Green Bay. Known for its delectable dishes and being a favorite of Aaron Rodgers, Renowned for their exceptional cuts of meat and fish, Chives Restaurant guarantees a dining experience that will leave your taste buds satisfied.

You might wonder, "What sets China Kitchen apart?" Well, as you approach this delightful eatery, a sign beckons you with a promise: "Our food has the lowest price, the highest value, the best taste, and larger portions. Please give it a try." While some might see this as a bold claim, let me tell you, it lives up to the hype!

With multiple visits under my belt, I can confidently say that China Kitchen has never disappointed. Their affordability is unmatched, as my wife and I consistently enjoy a delicious meal along with an egg roll for around $20. The food is always fresh, with portion sizes beyond what we can comfortably finish.

For those looking for authentic Chinese cuisine, China Kitchen is a great spot. Offering a strong combination of price, value, taste, and portion sizes, it truly delivers a great experience that won't break the bank.

So, whether you're a local or a curious traveler, venture off the beaten path and find your way to China Kitchen. You won't regret discovering this affordable and mouthwatering Chinese food joint in Green Bay. Give it a try and savor the goodness!
