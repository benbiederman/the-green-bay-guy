Sara's Artisan Gelato

If you're near Lambeau Field or in the Green Bay area, treat yourself to the delightful flavors of Sara's Artisan Gelato. As the name suggests, this gem is renowned for its handcrafted gelato and scrumptious sorbet, making it a must-visit spot.

Step into Sara's Artisan Gelato, conveniently located just off Oneida Street, and you'll immediately notice the pristine and inviting ambiance. The clean and welcoming space is accentuated by tasteful decor, providing the perfect backdrop for snapping Insta-worthy photos of your gelato, should you wish to share your sweet delights with the world.

What sets Sara's Artisan Gelato apart is their commitment to using only the finest ingredients, ensuring a premium taste in every spoonful. With an extensive selection of gelato and sorbet options, there's something to satisfy every palate. During my own delightful visit, I couldn't resist trying their Halloween candy gelato flavor. Opting for the warm brownie pairing was a superb choice, but beware: the portion size is deceiving; the combination of velvety gelato and luscious brownie proved almost too rich to conquer. Nevertheless, I powered through, as the taste was simply too irresistible to put down.

As a bonus, you'll find Sara's Artisan Gelato not only at its main location but also conveniently available at Angelina and the Titletown District. Additionally, you can bring the goodness home as they have their products stocked in various local grocery stores.

Whether you're a gelato aficionado or simply seeking a delightful treat, Sara's Artisan Gelato promises a memorable and mouthwatering experience for everyone. Plan your visit today and savor the finest artisan gelato and sorbet in Green Bay!
