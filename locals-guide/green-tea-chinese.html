Green Tea Chinese

Located on the East side of Green Bay, in an old Sonic building, is Green Tea Chinese. Green Tea Chinese is known for its quick service, reasonable prices, and delicious food. As a Chinese food enthusiast, I'm always excited to check out a new Chinese restaurant.

The first thing to note is that from the outside, Green Tea Chinese doesn’t look very inviting. It's housed in an old Sonic building. However, do not let the exterior deter you from giving it a try. The excellent service, competitive pricing, and tasty food more than make up for the storefront's appearance. One unique feature that Green Tea Chinese offers, thanks to its location in an old Sonic building, is a drive-thru. Green Tea Chinese maximizes this feature with impressively fast service, often outpacing traditional fast-food joints.

Despite being in a repurposed Sonic building, they still offer a few tables inside for customers who prefer to dine in. Every time I visit Green Tea Chinese, I'm pleasantly surprised by three things: how affordable it is, how fast the service is, and the generous portions. I have tried several different meals, and each time my food is ready by the time I get my water and napkins. Initially, I thought their speed was due to pre-made dishes, but it seems everything is quickly and freshly prepared to order.

The only potential downside of dining in at Green Tea Chinese, if I had to mention one, is the occasional noise from the drive-thru speaker. While this is a minor issue, I mention it for those who might find it bothersome. In that case, opting for takeout or using the drive-thru might be better. Regardless, the busy drive-thru is a testament to how popular and efficient their service is.

Overall, I’m a big fan of Green Tea Chinese. If you’re visiting Green Bay and looking for great Chinese food, be sure to check them out. They are a hidden gem in the city.
