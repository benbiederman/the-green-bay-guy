Taqueria Maldonados

Located on the east side of Green Bay is Taqueria Malonados, a restaurant known for its authentic Mexican dishes and hefty portions. Green Bay offers a variety of Mexican options, so I was excited to try one of the many they offer.

As we approached the restaurant, I was surprised by the size of Taqueria Malonados. There were just a handful of tables available for those who wanted to dine in, and only a couple of staff members were there to serve the guests. While no one was dining in during our visit, the restaurant was buzzing with people picking up takeout orders.

After briefly looking at the menu, I opted to try one of their signature dishes: the quesabirria tacos. After a short wait, we were served our food. My first impressions of the quesabirria tacos weren’t the greatest. Perhaps my experiences at La Carreta spoiled me, but visually, they seemed lacking. The tortillas didn’t look crisp; instead, they appeared dried out. As for the taste, their birria was enjoyable, but it didn’t match La Carreta's.

I will say that Taqueria Malonados receives a lot of praise and positive reviews online. So I’m not sure if we just had an off experience or if our expectations were too high because of this. Overall, our experience was okay, but I don’t think we’d be eager to return, especially with so many other great Mexican options in Green Bay.
