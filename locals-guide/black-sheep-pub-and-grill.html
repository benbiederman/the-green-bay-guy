Black Sheep Pub and Grill

Welcome to Black Sheep Pub & Grill, nestled on the outskirts of Green Bay, near the renowned UW GB. Whether you're a picky eater or a food enthusiast, this charming spot offers a delightful array of menu choices to suit every palate. Let's dive into the star of their menu that captured my attention and made it a must-try experience!

The moment I browsed their online menu, one item stood out: the irresistible Couch Potato Burger. Picture this – a mouthwatering cheeseburger paired with succulent pulled pork, crispy potato chips, and a tantalizing French onion dip. I simply couldn't resist; I knew I had to head over to Black Sheep Pub & Grill to savor this culinary masterpiece.

Before you embark on your culinary adventure, be prepared for a slight wait. This popular joint is always bustling with eager customers. However, don't fret! You can enjoy a refreshing drink at their cozy bar while you wait for your table.

Upon arriving at Black Sheep Pub & Grill, you'll have the delightful option of dining inside or soaking in the pleasant weather outside. During our visit, we chose to relish the outdoors in their lovely seating area, complete with an outdoor bar. The icing on the cake was the live music that evening, adding to the already inviting ambiance. I'm not sure if this is an exclusive Friday night treat, but it certainly set the mood for a fantastic evening. Plus, if you're in a more laid-back mood, they've got you covered with a fire pit and extra seating for you to unwind and enjoy a drink or two.

Finally, the much-anticipated moment arrived – my burger arrived at the table, and it was a sight to behold. The homemade touch, from the chips to the delectable French onion dip, elevated the taste to a whole new level. The only minor quibble was that the flavorful pulled pork slightly overshadowed the other ingredients in the burger. Nevertheless, with such an incredible culinary experience overall, it's easy to overlook this minor detail.

If you're in the Green Bay area and on the hunt for a fantastic spot to grab some grub or drinks, I highly recommend Black Sheep Pub & Grill. Their diverse menu and inviting atmosphere make it a delightful destination for locals and visitors alike. I, for one, am eagerly looking forward to my next visit!
