Fox Harbor

Looking for a place to enjoy a few drinks or some delicious food on a beautiful evening in Green Bay, Wisconsin? Look no further than Fox Harbor. Located on the Fox River, Fox Harbor offers a great place to dine or drink on their outdoor patio during those beautiful days in Green Bay or a sizeable interior for when it’s too nasty out to enjoy the weather.

If you’re planning a trip to Fox Harbor, my advice is to usually have a backup place to eat in mind too. Fox Harbor can get very busy. They don’t have a hostess or anything along those lines – it’s a seat-yourself situation, which can get a little messy when they are incredibly busy. However, if you’re opting to wait and figure out seating yourself, it’s not a bad decision either.

When it comes to the food, Fox Harbor offers a wide variety of delicious items. They pretty much have it all: burgers, sandwiches, wraps, fish, seafood, pizzas, salads, and even some options for the kiddos. Over the years of going to Fox Harbor, I’ve tried numerous items off their menu and have always had an enjoyable experience.

Fox Harbor has become a default place for my wife and me for lunch or dinner. We’ve always enjoyed the variety of food served, the location of the restaurant, and the service from the staff. We think you’d enjoy it as well if you’re looking for a delicious bite to eat – just get there early to avoid the craziness!
