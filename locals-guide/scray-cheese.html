Scray Cheese

When in Rome, do as the Romans do. When in Wisconsin, do as the Wisconsinites do - and eat cheese. Scray Cheese has been crafting incredible cheese in the Green Bay area for over 100 years, making it the perfect place for anyone looking to get some great cheese while in town.

Scray Cheese is located just outside of town and offers a quaint cheese shop for anyone needing their cheese fix. They provide a wide variety of cheese options, including cheese spreads, flavored cheeses, and premium aged cheeses. However, what I would encourage anyone to try at Scray Cheese is their fresh cheese curds.

Scray Cheese offers their fresh cheese curds every day they're open except for Fridays. Over the years, I've had a lot of cheese curds, and Scray Cheese's curds are some of my favorites. What I especially enjoy about Scray Cheese's cheese curds is that they are not overly salty. Often, fresh cheese curds can be a tad salty, and that saltiness can become overwhelming over time. However, Scray Cheese finds the perfect balance with their cheese curds.

Another great thing about Scray Cheese is that they offer some of their cheese for sale online. So, even if you're not in the area, you can still buy their cheese and have it quickly shipped to your house.

If you find yourself in the Green Bay area and are looking for a great place to pick up some fresh, high-quality cheese, look no further than Scray Cheese.
