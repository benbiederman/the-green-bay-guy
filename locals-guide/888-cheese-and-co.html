888 Cheese and Co.

Discover the joy of a scrumptious grilled cheese sandwich at 888 Cheese & Co., a delightful eatery outside of Green Bay that opened its doors in December 2021. The simplicity and deliciousness of their gourmet grilled cheese creations will leave you craving for more.

Upon entering 888 Cheese & Co., start by placing your order at the counter. I recommend reviewing their menu beforehand to be prepared when you reach the counter. If that's not possible, you can grab a menu from them and browse while waiting in line to place your order. After ordering, choose your seat, and they'll serve your food.

I also ordered a side of their pub chips, while my wife chose a sandwich and their tomato soup. Both sides complemented the sandwiches well, but the tomato soup was absolutely incredible. I'm not a soup connoisseur, but that was the best tomato soup I've ever had.

While dining at 888 Cheese and Co., we noticed they are next door to Ahnapee Brewery. Employees from 888 Cheese and Co. consistently delivered food to the brewery. If you're into craft beers and a delicious meal, you can order food from the brewery, and they will deliver it to you. It's a win-win!

888 Cheese and Co. will be a frequent dining spot for us. It offers the kind of meal that soothes your soul during a Wisconsin winter. If you're a grilled cheese enthusiast like us, I highly recommend giving them a try.
