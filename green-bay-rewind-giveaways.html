Green Bay Rewind Giveaway Rules and Guidelines

Effective date: 09/01/24

Welcome to the Green Bay Rewind Giveaway! We're excited to reward our loyal viewers with some amazing Packers memorabilia and other prizes. Please take a moment to read through the rules and guidelines to ensure you're eligible and understand how to participate.

1. Purpose of the Giveaway

The Green Bay Rewind Giveaway is our way of saying thanks to our loyal viewers. Each week, you have a chance to win some awesome Packers memorabilia just by tuning in and participating.

2. Eligibility

  • - Open to anyone 18 years and older.
  • - You must be able to receive mail to participate, as prizes will be shipped to winners.

3. Giveaway Dates

  • - The giveaway runs alongside our live show, Green Bay Rewind, every Tuesday at 7:30 PM, starting from September 10, 2024, and ending on February 11, 2025.
  • - Please note there will be no episodes or giveaways on December 24, 2024, and December 31, 2024.

4. How to Enter

  • - To enter, fill out the Google form linked at the start of each episode. Each episode will have a new form, so make sure to enter each week for a chance to win.
  • - You can enter once per episode.

5. Entry Limitations

  • - Only one entry per person, per episode is allowed.
  • - Additional entries or attempts to manipulate the entry process will result in disqualification.

6. Disqualification Criteria

You may be disqualified for any of the following:

  • - Being blocked or banned from the show's social media platforms.
  • - Spamming the entry form or comments.
  • - Providing false information.

7. Prizes

  • - Each week, we're giving away different pieces of Packers memorabilia, including signed 8x10s, signed jerseys, and gift cards to the Packers Pro Shop. The prizes are selected at random by the winner.
  • - The exact prize for each episode will be announced during the show.

8. Winner Selection and Announcement

  • - Winners will be chosen randomly during the live show. We'll draw names live and announce the winner right then and there!
  • - If you're announced as a winner, make sure to claim your prize by the end of the episode by getting my attention in the live chat.

9. Prize Claiming and Delivery

  • - If you win, it's your responsibility to claim the prize by engaging in the live chat during the episode.
  • - Prizes will be mailed to the winners. If you reside within the contiguous United States, we will cover the shipping costs. If you're outside of this area, we'll reach out via email to discuss how to get your prize to you.
  • After claiming your prize in the live show, please provide your shipping information within two weeks of receiving my email. If we don't receive your shipping details by then, the prize will be forfeited and added back to the prize pool for future episodes.
  • - Important: All communication regarding the delivery of your prize will be conducted via email from ben@thegreenbayguy.com only. I will never ask for payment information or other sensitive details in any other way. If you receive any communication about the giveaway from a different email address or through social media messages, it is not from us. Be cautious of spam bots and phishing scams that may try to trick you into providing personal information or money. Always verify the email address and contact ben@thegreenbayguy.com if you have any doubts.

10. Privacy Policy

  • - Your personal information is safe with us. It will solely be used to administer the giveaway and will not be shared or sold to any third parties.

11. Terms and Conditions

  • - By entering the giveaway, you agree to these rules and the terms laid out on this webpage.
  • -Nicolet Law, a proud sponsor of the show, supports these giveaways but is not responsible for them.

12. Questions or Support

  • - If you have any questions or need assistance regarding the giveaway, feel free to reach out to me at ben@thegreenbayguy.com.
